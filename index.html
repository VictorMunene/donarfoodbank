DONAR

We are a non-profitable organization that deals with the collection of donated foodstuffs and money to help feed the less fortunate within our community and also to spread our assistance and reach the ends of our land.
It would be a great honor and relief to help feed our fellow brothers and sisters in calamity sturck areas and show them that we are together despite challenging times.

With the donations, these struggling families and individuals may be able to get something to eat and drink and with this it may also help in battling some illnesses such as malnourishment and different deficiency diseases.

Aim

The aim of this organization is to reach out to restaurants, private parties or well-wishers in general to donate surplus foodstuffs that may be of use to the nourished souls.

Mission

To continually feed those unable to cater for themselves and bring a change and light to their lives.

Vision

To help eradicate world hunger by following and being in line with existing organizations and programmes to fight the same around the world.

Motto

Live in service, bless the needy.

Partners and Sponsors:

What does being a partner or sponsor entail?

The title partners and sponsors are very important and help the organization get funds and/or means of sharing the food donated from the well-wishers to the less fortunate.
The various parties help in the blessing of the nourished souls and in return, they get publicity and build a good rapport to the rest of the people. They are also included in such an organization as Donar that may be in their special programmes that help connect their customers to a legitimate organization that they may wish to join or help.
